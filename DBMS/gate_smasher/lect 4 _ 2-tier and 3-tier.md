
# **2-tier**
1} known as client-server architecture 
2} slow as compare to 3 layer architecture 
3} the client request will go to the d.b and then d.b manages the request and give the response 



# 3-tier
1} client - business - data base  architecture .
2} application layer handle all the requests , convert it into low level language and then give it to d.b . 
3} d.b give things back to business layer ,  then to client 
4} it has dedicated step , which process the logic .
